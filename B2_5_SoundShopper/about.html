Parts of our sites use a browser feature called "cookies". A cookie is a small data
file that certain Web sites write to your hard drive when you visit them. A cookie file
can contain information such as a user ID that the site uses to track the pages
you've visited or keep track of what you've put in a shopping basket. The only personal
information a cookie can contain is information you supply yourself. A cookie can't read
data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. A cookie will not
corrupt your hard drive or transmit viruses to your computer.
